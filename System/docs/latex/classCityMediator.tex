\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Acts as a mediator for coordinating actions within the city. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCityMediator}{City\+Mediator}} class facilitates communication between the city and its tiles, reacting to changes in tile states and providing city services like power, water, and waste management.
