\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a water tile in the simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classWater}{Water}} class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classTile}{Tile}} and defines properties and behaviors specific to water tiles, which may be used to represent natural water bodies or resources within the simulation.
