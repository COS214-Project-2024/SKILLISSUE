Represents a water treatment plant tile in the simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classWaterPlant}{Water\+Plant}} class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classTile}{Tile}} and defines properties and behaviors specific to water treatment plants, which are responsible for managing and supplying water resources within the simulation.
