\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Handles population distribution within the map. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classDReceiver}{DReceiver}} class is responsible for managing the distribution of population and employment across different tiles in the map, applying birth and death rates and adjusting pools as needed.
